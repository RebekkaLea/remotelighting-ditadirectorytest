List of manned moon landings

There have been six crewed moon landings, all part of NASA's Apollo program.

The missions and their landing dates are:

  • Apollo 11 - July 20, 1969
  • Apollo 12 - November 19, 1969
  • Apollo 14 - February 5, 1971
  • Apollo 15 - July 30, 1971
  • Apollo 16 - April 21, 1972
  • Apollo 17 - December 11, 1972

NASA's Artemis III mission is currently scheduled for September 2026. This will be the first crewed landing on the Moon since Apollo 17 in 1972.
